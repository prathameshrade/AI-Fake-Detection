System Overview

How This Detector Thinks

SignalScope combines artifact heuristics and score calibration to produce interpretable deepfake risk estimates.

Image

Measures blur, texture frequency balance, and compression block boundaries to catch synthetic visual artifacts.

Audio

Checks spectral flatness, zero-crossing regularity, and spectral variability for cloned-voice signatures.

Video

Aggregates frame-level scores and temporal consistency signals to identify motion and rendering anomalies.

Brief Analysis Output

Every prediction includes a short narrative summary so users can quickly understand whether suspicious patterns were weak, moderate, or strong.
